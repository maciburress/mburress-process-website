Solution

After completing the design challenge, researching the organization, figuring out the audience and goals, the solution for the website emerged in the form of a style tile.

Style Tiles

Style tiles are a way to display the "essence" of a webesite. It inlcudes typefaces, colors, and interface elements that present possible solutions for the website. For my organization, I have created a style tile that shows what the website could be. This style tile displays warm and inviting colors, moods the executive director of the New Day Clubhouse wanted to invoke.

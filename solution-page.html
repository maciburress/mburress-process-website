Solution

After completing the design challenge, researching the organization, figuring out the audience and goals, the solution for the website emerged in the form of a style tile.

Style Tiles

Style tiles are a way to display the "essence" of a webesite. It inlcudes typefaces, colors, and interface elements that present possible solutions for the website. For my organization, I have created a style tile that shows what the website could be. This style tile displays warm and inviting colors, moods the executive director of the New Day Clubhouse wanted to invoke. The final website should evoke a feeling of warmth and calmness while displaying an organized layout, concise information, and easy navigation that as a whole presents professionalism.

Call to Action

The call to action is, arguably, the most important element on a site. The call to action I chose for the New Day Clubhouse is, "Become a Member." I chose this CTA because it is the one purpose of the clubhouse, for mentally ill individuals to choose to become members and better their lives. That is the action the organization wants from the viewer.
